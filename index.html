DH Builders

DH Builders is a family run building company. We specialise in loft conversions, extensions and new builds and often carry out full renovation works for clients.

We offer a range of services including roofing slate and tile, lead work, torch on felt systems, single ply, rubber roofing, painting, decorating, repair work, brick work and more.

We are based in Romsey, however we work across the south. We offer free quotes for any work and our rates are competitive. We strive to get every job done to the standard you desire.
